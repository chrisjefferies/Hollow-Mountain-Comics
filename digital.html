All Your Pulls & Subscriptions.

All from wherever you're reading this.

Hollow Mountain has partnered with ComiXology to bring you the convenience of managing all of your comic orders, pulls and subscriptions online.

Simply Connect with Hollow Mountain on ComiXology (once you've created a free account) and we'll add your pull list to our weekly comic order (after we contact you to verify your humanity). Once that's done, all your comics will reside behind the desk to be picked up at your convenience.

Connecting to Hollow Mountain

Click "Connect on ComiXology" in the dropdown of our Nav bar under Digital.

Click here

The window that pops up will be our retailer page on ComiXology. Click the Connect button, and if you aren't already, sign in to your account or create one.

Simply browse the ComiXology inventory, select your favorite titles and add them to your pull list. If you subscribe to a weekly comic and subsequent issues will automatically be added to your pull list.
